Professional Development Program (PDP)

For existing teachers in schools

Modules Overview

Academic Leadership & Policy
  • School Management and Leadership ( For HM and Principals)
    Build administrative vision and leadership skills for effective school functioning.
  • Design Your Career
    Guide teachers in self-mapping professional growth and lifelong learning goals.
  • National Education Policy and Institutional Building
    Understand NEP 2020 and its impact on curriculum, pedagogy, and assessment practices.

Pedagogy & Teaching Innovation
  • Enhancing Teaching & Learning Experiences
    Apply real-life teaching frameworks, understand generational learners, and explore the Learner’s Pit.
  • ClassCommand – Mastering Classroom Management
    Strengthen positive behavior systems, rules, routines, and engagement strategies.
  • Design Thinking for Educators
    Apply empathy-driven and problem-solving approaches to improve instruction and student connection.

Life Skills, Counselling & Emotional Wellness
  • Mentoring and Counselling Skills for Teachers
    Equip teachers to be approachable guides, managing student emotions and crises empathetically.
  • Mind Education
    Develop emotional resilience and calm mindset practices to manage stress effectively—for both teachers and students.
  • Guidance and Counselling in Schools
    Identify learning needs and behavioral issues and direct students toward supportive interventions.
  • Developing Life Skills
    Integrate soft skills, such as empathy, decision-making, and self-awareness, into everyday teaching.

Communication & Engagement
  • Communicative and Interactive Skills
    Cultivate verbal clarity, storytelling techniques, questioning skills, and student participation strategies.
  • Educational Communication Technologies
    Learn to use tech tools (interactive boards, apps, podcasts) for better classroom engagement.
  • Fluent Teacher
    Develop classroom English fluency, clear communication, and confidence in instruction.

Digital & Technological Competence
  • E-Content Development
    Train teachers to design effective digital lessons using visuals, videos, and audio.
  • Education Technology
    Navigate educational platforms, online assessments, learning analytics, and blended models.

Early Years Specialization
  • Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE)
    Understand developmental milestones, learning through play, classroom setup, and foundational literacy/numeracy.
  • Foundations of Heutagogy
    Explore self-directed learning and how teachers can facilitate learning independence in young learners.

Pedagogical Innovation
  • Multilevel & Multigrade Teaching
    For teachers in rural/small schools managing mixed-age classrooms.
  • Inclusive Education Practices
    Adapting content for learners with diverse abilities (special needs, learning difficulties, etc.).
  • Assessment for Learning (AfL)
    Move beyond summative tests — formative, diagnostic, and feedback-driven assessment strategies.
  • Project-Based & Inquiry-Based Learning
    Hands-on, real-world learning models that promote critical thinking.
  • Remedial & Differentiated Instruction
    Techniques to support struggling learners and fast finishers in the same class.

Teacher Well-being & Emotional Intelligence
  • Teacher Wellness & Burnout Prevention
    Stress management, work-life balance, mindfulness, and personal reflection.
  • Emotional Intelligence in Teaching
    Understanding emotions, triggers, self-regulation, and empathy as teaching tools.

Digital Teaching & AI Integration
  • AI for Educators
    How to use tools like ChatGPT, Canva Magic Write, and AI lesson generators responsibly.
  • Gamification in the Classroom
    Tools like Kahoot, Blooket, and Quizizz to enhance engagement.
  • Cyber Safety & Digital Citizenship for Teachers
    Online etiquette, protecting student data, and managing digital behavior.
  • Data-Driven Teaching
    Use of student performance data to drive instruction and remediation.

Soft Skills & Professionalism
  • Public Speaking & Presentation Skills for Teachers
    Build voice modulation, storytelling, body language, and confident presentation.
  • Conflict Management & Difficult Conversations
    How to manage conflicts with students, parents, and colleagues professionally.
  • Time Management & Task Prioritization
    Balancing lesson planning, grading, and responsibilities efficiently.
  • Teacher as Facilitator, Not Instructor
    Shift from knowledge-giver to coach/guide using constructivist models.

Parent & Community Engagement
  • Effective Parent-Teacher Communication
    Conducting meetings, giving feedback, and handling complaints respectfully.
  • Community-Connected Classrooms
    Bringing local knowledge, guest speakers, and real-life examples into learning.

Policy & Ethics
  • Child Protection & Safeguarding in Schools
    Understanding the POCSO Act, identifying signs of abuse, and safe reporting.
  • Ethical & Reflective Teaching Practices
    Building integrity, equity, and accountability in teaching.



Who Should Attend?

  • Primary and Secondary School Teachers
  • Subject Coordinators
  • Academic Leaders
  • Aspiring School Heads

Suggested Delivery Model

  • Mode: Onsite or Blended
  • Duration per module: 3–6 hours
  • Frequency: Bi-monthly / Term-wise
  • Resources: Trainer manual, session toolkit, reflection journal

Certification & Recognition
