Welcome to Our ACCESS

ACCESS is a professional academic consulting and school support organization committed to strengthening teachers, empowering students, and enabling schools to deliver consistent, high-quality education.

We provide end-to-end educational solutions through teacher outsourcing and recruitment, teacher training programs, soft skills curriculum with trainer deployment, student development initiatives, and academic consulting for schools and colleges.

ACCESS was founded to address one of the biggest challenges in Indian education — the shortage of trained, classroom-ready, and professional teachers. We solve this gap by recruiting talented graduates, rigorously training them through our Teacher Readiness Program (TRP), and deploying them as confident, skilled, and student-focused educators.

Our flagship strength is Teacher Outsourcing, powered by ACCESS-trained teachers who are equipped with strong communication skills, effective classroom management techniques, modern teaching methodologies, and NEP-aligned pedagogical practices. These teachers are not just placed in schools — they are continuously supported, monitored, and developed to ensure sustained classroom excellence.

Beyond teachers, ACCESS also partners with schools to build future-ready students through structured soft skills curriculum, personality development programs, and student-focused training sessions. Our academic consulting services further support school leadership with curriculum guidance, teacher performance improvement, and quality enhancement strategies.

At ACCESS, we believe quality education begins with quality teachers. Our purpose is simple yet powerful:

Empowering Schools. Transforming Classrooms. Enabling Success.

Founder’s Note

ACCESS is founded and led by Surya Venkatesh, an education leader with extensive, on-ground experience in teacher training, curriculum design, soft skills development, academic consulting, and student transformation. His work spans classrooms, training halls, and school leadership forums, with a clear focus on strengthening the foundations of education rather than offering surface-level solutions.

Through years of direct engagement with teachers, students, and school leadership teams, Surya observed a recurring reality in Indian education: schools do not struggle due to lack of effort or intent, but due to the absence of trained, classroom-ready teachers and structured, continuous student development frameworks. While academic content is often delivered, students frequently lack confidence, communication skills, practical life readiness, and clarity about themselves and their future. This gap became the driving force behind the creation of ACCESS.

ACCESS was established with a clear and uncompromising purpose: to build strong teachers and develop well-rounded students. The organization works by training and deploying classroom-ready teachers, delivering professional teacher training programs, implementing student motivation and personality development initiatives, rolling out structured soft skills curriculum, and supporting schools through academic consulting.

Surya’s vision is firm and student-centered: every classroom must be led by a confident, well-trained teacher, and every student should leave school not only with strong subject knowledge, but also with practical life skills, clear communication, emotional strength, civic responsibility, and clarity of thought and direction. Education must create good citizens and capable individuals, not just exam performers. ACCESS exists to help schools move beyond syllabus completion and build learners who are prepared for higher education, work, and real life.

“Great schools are built on strong teachers and confident learners.”

— Surya Venkatesh, Founder – ACCESS
